Indonesian Cultures

Here is the culture from Indonesia

Wayang is a performance art played by a puppeteer accompanied by gamelan music and the voice of a singer. The story told in the puppet play about Petruk, Semar, Bagong, and Gareng.

Angklung is a traditional musical instrument from West Java which is made from bamboo. The instrument has also received recognition from UNESCO as part of World Heritage on January 19, 2011.

Keris is a traditional weapon believed to contain magical powers. Keris was recognized by UNESCO as the 'Masterpiece of the Oral and Intangible Heritage of Humanity'.

Reog Ponorogo is an Indonesian culture originating from the East Java region. Reog players wear masks in the shape of a lion's head with a crown made of peacock feathers.

Bali is a tourist spot that is well known abroad. Its well-known culture is the Kecak Dance. The Kecak dance uses the screams of 'cak cak to cak cak ke' as accompaniment music.

Batik is a pictorial cloth which is specially made by writing or lighting the cloth with a night, then the processing is processed in a certain way which has its own characteristics.
